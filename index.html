Whoop Setup Guide
›
1
Create developer account
Go to developer.whoop.com and sign in with your Whoop account. Create a new app called "Baseline".
2
Set redirect URI
In your app settings, set the redirect URI to: https://baseline.app/callback (or your Netlify URL). Copy your Client ID and Client Secret.
3
Enter credentials below
Paste your Client ID here. The full OAuth connection requires a backend — coming in the next build. For now your credentials are saved locally.
